How to Read the Scores

A score closer to zero suggests a better match of the text to the language; Log-probability was used, hence scores are negative.

A larger top-vs-next score gap suggests that the prediction is more certain.

Key Features

  • Character-level 4-grams capture local language patterns.
  • Add-one smoothing keeps probability calculations stable for unseen grams.
  • Log-probability scoring and threshold checks support reliable fallback behaviour of unseen terms.
